King Rajasinghe I and the Seethawaka Kingdom.

Seethawaka Kingdom started in 1521 AD as a result of the incidence called "Vijayaba Kollaya". After Vijayaba Kollaya Kotte Kingdom divided into three kingdoms as Kotte, Seethawaka and Raigama. So, King Buwanekabahu VII took the control of Kotte Kingdom. And King Mayadunne took the control of Seethawaka Kingdom and King Raigam Bandara took the control of Raigama Kingdom. However, King Raigam Bandara passed away in 1547 AD during the reign of King Mayadunne of Seethawaka and after the death of King Raigam Bandara, King Mayadunne annexed Raigama Kingdom to Seethawaka Kingdom. And his son named "Tikiri Bandara" also was a great warrior. Since his childhood He defeated Portuguese at the Mulleriyawa battle and defeated Veediya Bandara who was known as Tiger of Kotte. However, King Mayadunne died and Prince Tikiri became the king of Seethawaka as King Rajasinghe I in 1581 AD. After a few days after becoming the king, he was able to chase King Karaliyadde Bandara away and to annex Kandyan Kingdom to Seethawaka. He was a great warrior but, there were some bad qualities of him too. at the last time period of his reign, he did many anti-buddhist work according to a wrong advice of his Hindu advisor to him. Because of that, his his power also became weak and that brought the end of Seethawaka Kingdom.
